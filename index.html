Throughout working at In-N-Out, I received many new skills, as well as earning 4 promotions that allowed me to work around the second highest level for employees my age. The skills I acquired were excellent customer service, managing cash registers and transactions, taking food orders and handing them out, and preparing and serving food in the kitchen. Although it was a seasonal job, I learned many valuable skills that allowed me to be who I am today in the working world.

Since I did many activities I was able to manage my time very well with hard work and responsibility.
